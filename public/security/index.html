Security

How to report issues and what we prioritize.

Last updated: 2025-12-12

Reporting

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please email diwakar@26weeks.ai with details and reproduction steps. Please do not publicly disclose issues before we've had a chance to investigate.

What We Ask

  • Provide a clear description, impact, and steps to reproduce.
  • Only test against accounts or systems you own or have permission to assess.
  • Avoid actions that degrade service availability for others.

Our Approach

  • Prioritize fixes based on severity and exploitability.
  • Limit access using least-privilege principles where applicable.
  • Rotate credentials/secrets promptly if exposure is suspected.
